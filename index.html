SELLING AND WITHDRAWAL RULES

  • Users can sell tokens equivalent to 1% of their total invested capital (in token value) daily.
  • The invested capital is locked for a user-selected locking period (e.g., 3, 6, 12, or 24 months).
  • Proceeds from daily token sales are accumulated and can be withdrawn weekly (e.g., every Sunday).
  • Minimum withdrawal threshold is 50 USDT to optimize transaction fees.
  • Smart contract enforces the 1% daily selling limit, tracks the locking period, and manages weekly withdrawals.

REFERRAL PROGRAM

  • Users earn a 10% bonus in USDT for every direct referral who invests in the presale.
  • Users earn a 5% bonus in USDT for investments made by second-level referrals (referrals of direct referrals).
  • Referral bonuses are credited weekly and can be withdrawn during the weekly withdrawal cycle.
  • Each user receives a unique referral link/code to share with others.
  • Bonuses are funded from a designated referral pool (e.g., 5% of total presale funds).
